Frequently Asked Questions

What does FreshPlate do?

FreshPlate is a pantry tracking app that helps you keep track of everything in your fridge, freezer, and cupboards. It shows you what you have, what's expiring soon, and suggests recipes you can make with what's already at home — so you waste less food and spend less at the grocery store.

How do I add items to my pantry?

You can add items by scanning a barcode with your camera or searching by name. FreshPlate pulls in the product name, category, and nutritional info automatically. You can also set a quantity and expiration date at the same time.

How do expiry alerts work?

When you add an item, you can set its expiration date. FreshPlate will send you a notification a few days before it expires so you have time to use it up. You can adjust how early you get notified in the app's settings.

Can FreshPlate suggest recipes from my pantry?

Yes — FreshPlate looks at what you currently have and suggests recipes that use those ingredients. You can filter by meal type, dietary preferences, and more. It's a great way to use up what you have before buying more.

How does the grocery list work?

FreshPlate generates a grocery list based on what you're running low on or missing for a recipe. It automatically skips items you already have in your pantry, so you never buy duplicates by accident.

Can I share my pantry with family or roommates?

Yes. You can share your pantry with other FreshPlate users so everyone in your household sees the same inventory in real time. Anyone can add, update, or remove items.

Can I use FreshPlate in a different language?

Yes — FreshPlate supports English, Spanish, French, Hindi, Portuguese, Arabic, Chinese (Simplified), and German. You can choose your preferred language during onboarding when you first sign up, or change it later in Profile → Settings → Appearance → Language. The app will restart briefly to apply your chosen language.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → FreshPlate. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period with no charges after that.
